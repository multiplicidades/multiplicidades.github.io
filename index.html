Who is right?

Choreographer
Chloe Beilleveire, assistant Francisca Crisóstomo López.

Performers
Henar Garcia Herranz, Florian Astraudo, Małgorzata Piastowska, Andreia Serrada, Natacha Campos, Victor Gomes, Paulina Będkowska, Leon Casper Stille, Gabriela Bondone, Katarzyna Paluch, Isadora Franco, Evie Cousins, Opazo Manuela, Juliana Fernandes, Aaron Lang.

Duration
40 min

Description
Truth here, another truth there…. Most of the concepts that we have in mind work in the duality of opposites: true and false, abstract and concrete, good and evil, heaven and hell, pain and pleasure, initiatory death and rebirth. The contradictions are inseparable, complementary and fight at the same time. We have a tendency to approach life and various situations with a radical or a moderate point of view... We put in question where is the balance in all of that and try to observe the complexity of human relationship.

Put your helmets on!

Choreographer
Sara Grauls

Performers
Sara Grauls, Evie Cousins

Duration
20 min.

Description
Wearing a Motorcylce Helmet distorts our perception of what we like to call 'reality': We only see what is right in front of us, and we only hear that which is close by. Our conditioned mind seems to be no different: we only see what we are programmed to see- ... as we only hear what we are conditioned to hear. 'Put Your Helmets On!' is a research about the effects a distorted vision and hearing have on the performers, as a metaphor for the distortions we perceive due to our conditioned minds.
